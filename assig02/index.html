My Wacky Favorites

Hummus
A dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. It is popular in the Middle East and Mediterranean, as well as in Middle Eastern cuisine around the globe. It can also be found in most grocery stores in North America and Europe. It is one of the most delicious creations ever to grace the world.
Overnight Oats
Overnight oats is an alternative way of preparing oatmeal. Overnight oats are mixed with milk and left overnight in the fridge, allowing the oats and seeds to hydrate. While you're sleeping, overnight oats absorb the liquid, soften up, and form a texture that makes for a great breakfast food. Add in some extras, like chia seeds, Greek yogurt, fruit, vanilla, flax, or peanut butter.
Potjiekos
In South Africa, a potjiekos /ˈpɔɪkiːkɒs/, literally translated "small-pot food", is a dish prepared outdoors. It is traditionally cooked in a round, cast iron, three-legged pot, the potjie, descended from the Dutch oven brought from the Netherlands to South Africa in the 17th century and found in the homes and villages of people throughout southern Africa.
